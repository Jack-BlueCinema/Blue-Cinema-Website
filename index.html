Blue Cinema

Founded in 2022 by Malka (vocals) and Gigi (guitar), Blue Cinema is an electrifying fusion of shoegaze, dreampop, synth-pop, psychedelic, and new wave, coined affectionately as "Shoe-Synth-Dream."

This Cambridge-based quintet crafts immersive soundscapes that transport listeners to ethereal realms, blending lush melodies with mesmerizing textures and hypnotic rhythms.

Annie (keys), J Mass (drums), and Jack (bass) complete the lineup, each contributing their unique talents to the band's sonic tapestry. Drawing inspiration from luminaries such as Cocteau Twins, My Bloody Valentine, and New Order, Blue Cinema creates a sonic journey that is both exhilarating and introspective.

Blue Cinema's debut EP, Passages, is a testament to their distinctive sound and artistic vision. Their most famous song, Cerebro, stands as a testament to their knack for crafting immersive sonic landscapes that resonate with listeners.

In 2024, Blue Cinema achieved a significant milestone by supporting the legendary Miki Berenyi Trio, renowned pioneers of the shoegaze genre. This opportunity not only showcased their talent to a wider audience but also solidified their position as rising stars in the shoegaze world.

With their captivating live performances and genre-defying sound, Blue Cinema continues to push the boundaries of alternative music while captivating audiences with their unique blend of nostalgia and innovation.

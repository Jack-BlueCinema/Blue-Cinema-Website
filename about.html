Blue Cinema

Blue Cinema is a Cambridge-based quintet formed in 2022 by Malka (vocals) and Gigi (guitar), crafting a hypnotic blend of shoegaze, dreampop, synth-pop, psychedelic and new wave, often described as “shoe-synth-dream”.

Built around immersive textures, lush melodies and driving rhythms, the band features Annie (keys), Jack (bass) and J Mass (drums), each shaping their layered, atmospheric sound.

Drawing influence from artists such as Cocteau Twins, My Bloody Valentine and New Order, Blue Cinema balance nostalgia and intensity with a modern edge, creating expansive soundscapes that sit between dream and distortion.

Live, they’ve quickly built momentum across the UK scene, including standout performances supporting Miki Berenyi Trio and Chastity Belt, appearances at Shiny New Model Festival alongside artists such as Antony Szmierek, Dream Wife and Night Tapes, and regular shows across Cambridge, London and beyond including UK shoegaze and alternative festival lineups.

Their debut EP 'Passages' introduces their signature sound, while tracks such as 'Cerebro' showcase their ability to build immersive sonic worlds.

Blue Cinema continue to evolve within the UK alternative scene, carving out a space between shoegaze revivalism and contemporary dream-pop experimentation.
